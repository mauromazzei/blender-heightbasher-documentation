Heightbasher Documentation

Heightbasher is an addon for Blender 2.8+, a creative tool dedicated to concept artists that gives you the opportunity to create 3D concepts through a fast and non-destructive workflow thanks to a real-time rendering of multiple heightmaps drawn in a 2d environment
